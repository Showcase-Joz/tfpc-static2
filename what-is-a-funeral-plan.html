What is a funeral plan?

With a funeral plan, you plan for the funeral you would like. Pre-paying the costs of your funeral at today’s prices. This ensures you get greater value from your funeral planning. Helping you understand that preparation for this life event is complete.

You simply plan and pay for your funeral in advance.

How it works...

With The Funeral Planning Company, your payments are all protected. Our prepaid funeral plans are underwritten by the Pride Planning Trust. Guaranteed by the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000, Article 60. Making your funeral arrangements today. Will mean your loved ones don’t have to worry in the future. With your family upset grieving when you die. It wouldn't be the best time to make an informed decision, would it? It makes sense for you to deal with your own funeral in advance. That’s how funeral plans came about! From the need to stay in control of your current and future situations.

The Pride Planning Trust is held by Barclays bank and is managed by an independent panel of qualified Trustees who, along with independent key professionals to include; International fund manager Investec, qualified Actuary Rowanmoor, award winning Chartered Accountants and Business Advisors Kay Johnson Gee (KJG LLP), and one of the UK’s top 100 law firms Gordons Solicitors, manage the Trust’s funds on behalf of yourselves, our plan holders safeguarding future payments to Funeral Directors.

The purpose of the Pride Planning Trust is to safeguard your money, and is independent to the funeral plan provider. By being so, your money is totally safeguarded, and when the time comes, the Funeral Directors will receive payment direct from the Trust.

The Golden Leaves Trust independently manages the funds to achieve stable long-term growth, specifically covering the increased cost of funeral services. The Trust is regulated by The Financial Services and Markets Act 2001, and is overseen by a board of trustees, who have appointed Julius Bär and Quilter Cheviot as the investment fund manager for the Trust, and Lloyds TSB as the fund’s banker.

The fund is annually and independently audited subject to strict actuarial reporting, ensuring it remains solid, stable and, completely secure.
